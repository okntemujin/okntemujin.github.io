About

I am a PhD student in Chemistry at the University of California, Berkeley. I aspirations of pursuing research in computational chemistry and quantum chemistry.

Recently, I have graduated with a B.S. in Chemistry & Biology and Physics at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. During my undergraduate studies, I have been involved in various research projects, including electronic structure method development, algorithm design for organic transformations, and data-efficient machine learning of chemical properties.

I have also been actively involved in mentoring and teaching, serving as a tutor for several MIT chemistry courses and leading review sessions for exams and assignments.

Outside of chemistry, I have a strong interest in software development and design. I have some experience in building websites and light chemical software. I plan to continue developing my skills in this area.
